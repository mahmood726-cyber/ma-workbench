Methodology

Seed: 20260415 (xoshiro128**). N=10,000 replications. True log-HR ∼ Uniform(log 0.6, log 1.3). Per-trial SE ∼ Uniform(0.04, 0.08). Fixed-effects inverse-variance pool on log-HR scale. At each dp, both HR and 95% CI bounds are rounded; SE is re-derived from the rounded CI — matches real-paper extraction.
