Conjugate model: yi ∼ N(θ, vi+τ²), θ ∼ N(μ₀, τ₀²). Posterior: θ | data ∼ N(μ*, τ*²) with τ*² = (1/τ₀² + Σ 1/(vi+τ²))−1. Posterior probabilities come from the Gaussian CDF.
